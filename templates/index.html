Geometric morphometric pipeline modernised over Criscione & Patti (2010, The Nautilus 124(3):117–128). Built-in Rissoidae template (19 landmarks; apex, three last whorls, aperture). Updated with sliding semi-landmarks (Gunz, Mitteroecker & Bookstein 2005), Procrustes ANOVA via RRPP (Collyer & Adams 2018), Elliptic Fourier outline analysis (Bonhomme et al. 2014), Common Allometric Component (Mitteroecker et al. 2004), and Kmult phylogenetic signal (Adams 2014).
All-in-one replacement for tpsDig2 + tpsRelw + SPSS + SYSTAT.
